About the HDA

Our history, mission, and the people behind the league

Our History

The Harare Darts Association (HDA) is the premier darts organization in Harare, Zimbabwe. Established to promote and organize competitive darts across the city, the HDA brings together players from pubs, clubs, and community venues in a spirit of friendly rivalry and sportsmanship.

Over the years, the league has grown to encompass multiple teams competing across various venues, with regular weekly fixtures, exciting tournaments, and a passionate community of players and fans.

Our Mission

To foster the growth and development of darts as a competitive sport in Harare, providing a well-organized league structure, fair play, and opportunities for players of all skill levels to participate and excel.
